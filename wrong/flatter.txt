flatter

[ADJECTIVE] having a level surface; without raised areas or indentations.
[ADJECTIVE] lacking emotion; dull and lifeless.
[ADJECTIVE] (of a sparkling drink) having lost its effervescence.
[ADJECTIVE] (of a fee, wage, or price) the same in all cases, not varying with changed conditions or in particular cases.
[ADJECTIVE] (of musical sound) below true or normal pitch.
[ADJECTIVE] relating to flat racing.
[VERB] lavish praise and compliments on (someone), often insincerely and with the aim of furthering one's own interests.

slick

[ADJECTIVE] done or operating in an impressively smooth and efficient way.
[ADJECTIVE] (of skin or hair) smooth and glossy.
[NOUN] an oil slick.
[NOUN] an application or amount of a glossy or oily substance.
[VERB] make (one's hair) flat, smooth, and glossy by applying water, oil, or gel to it.
[NOUN] a racing-car or bicycle tyre without a tread, for use in dry weather conditions.

scrap

[NOUN] a small piece or amount of something, especially one that is left over after the greater part has been used.
[VERB] discard or remove from service (a redundant, old, or inoperative vehicle, vessel, or machine), especially so as to convert it to scrap metal.
[NOUN] a fight or quarrel, especially a minor or spontaneous one.
[VERB] engage in a minor fight or quarrel.
[NOUN] discarded metal for reprocessing.
